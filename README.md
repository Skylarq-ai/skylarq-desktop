## One agent replaces your entire sales stack

Skylarq runs locally on your Mac as a native desktop app. Your data stays private, the agent runs 24/7 in the background, and there are no browser tabs to manage.

It handles prospecting, outreach, meeting notes, CRM updates, and signal tracking — so you can focus on closing deals instead of switching between tools.
